Pollution and Disease correlation analysis

In this project we analyzed the correlation between various air pollutants and different diseases. We used statistical methods to identify potential predictors of disease incidence based on air quality data. The analysis involved collecting data from multiple sources, cleaning and preprocessing the data, and applying correlation analysis techniques to uncover significant relationships. We used data gathered from areas around USA, since it had the most comprehensive datasets available.

Key Takeaways

  • Worked with non-normally distributed data
  • Gained experience in data collection and preprocessing
  • Learned to apply statistical methods for correlation analysis
  • Improved skills in data visualization and interpretation of results

Technologies used

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy
